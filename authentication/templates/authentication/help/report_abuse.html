REPORT ABUSE

Your reports help us keep Roblox safe for everyone. If you've experienced or witnessed inappropriate behavior, content, or violations of our Community Standards, please report it immediately.

In case of immediate danger, contact local authorities first

WHAT CAN YOU REPORT?

We review all reports and take appropriate action

Inappropriate Behavior

Report users who engage in harmful or disruptive conduct

  • Bullying, harassment, or threats
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Sexual content or advances
  • Spam or scamming attempts

Inappropriate Content

Flag content that violates our community standards

  • Violent or disturbing games/images
  • Sexual or romantic content
  • Profanity or inappropriate text
  • Copyrighted material

Exploits & Cheating

Report players using unfair advantages

  • Hacking or exploiting
  • Using unauthorized modifications
  • Account theft or phishing
  • Botting or automation

Safety Concerns

Report serious safety or security issues

  • Child endangerment
  • Sharing personal information
  • Self-harm or suicide content
  • Real-world threats

Scams & Fraud

Report fraudulent activity or deception

  • Robux scams or fake giveaways
  • Phishing attempts
  • Fake trade offers
  • Account selling schemes

Impersonation

Report users pretending to be others

  • Impersonating Roblox staff
  • Fake celebrity accounts
  • Copying other users' identities
  • Misleading group names

HOW TO REPORT IN-GAME

The fastest way to report is directly within Roblox while the issue is happening

1

Find the Report Button

Look for the three dots menu or flag icon next to the user, content, or chat message

2

Select Report Type

Choose the category that best matches the violation from the dropdown menu

3

Provide Details

Briefly describe what happened. Include relevant context to help moderators review

4

Submit Report

Click submit and you'll receive a confirmation. Our team will review it promptly

SUBMIT A DETAILED REPORT

Use this form for comprehensive reports or if you're unable to report in-game

If reporting a user, include their username

Be specific and include dates/times if possible

Upload screenshots, recordings, or other evidence (Max 5 files, 10MB each)

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER YOU REPORT?

1

Report Received

Your report is immediately logged in our system and you'll receive a confirmation message

2

Review by Moderation Team

Our trained moderators review your report, gather evidence, and assess the violation severity

3

Action Taken

If a violation is confirmed, we take appropriate action (warning, suspension, or ban)

4

Follow-up (if needed)

For serious cases, we may contact you for additional information or to inform you of the outcome

STAY SAFE WHILE REPORTING

Important tips to remember when dealing with problematic users or content

Block First: Block the user immediately to prevent further contact
Don't Engage: Avoid responding or arguing with the offender
Save Evidence: Take screenshots before content is deleted
Tell a Trusted Adult: If you're a minor, inform a parent or guardian
Report Promptly: Report as soon as possible while details are fresh
Don't Share Personal Info: Never reveal personal details to strangers
