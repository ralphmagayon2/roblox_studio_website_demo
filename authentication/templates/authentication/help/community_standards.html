COMMUNITY STANDARDS

Our community standards ensure Roblox remains a safe, welcoming, and civil environment for everyone. These guidelines apply to all users, content, and behavior on the platform.

Last Updated: January 03, 2025
{% comment %} Introduction {% endcomment %}

OUR MISSION

Roblox's mission is to bring the world together though play. We envision a world where anyone can come together to create, share experiences, and express themselves in a safe and civil environement.

These Community Standards outline what and isn't allowed on Roblox. By using our platform, you agree to follow these standards. Violations may result in moderation action including warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans.

Safety & Security

We have zero tolerance for content or behavior that theatens real-world safety or security, including threats of violence, self-harm, exploitation, and illegal activites.

Prohibited:

  • Threats of violence or harm toward others
  • Sharing personal information (doxxing)
  • Content promoting self-harm or suicide
  • Child endangerment or exploitation
  • Terrorism or violent extremism
  • Illegal activities or substances

IMMEDIATE BAN

Civility & Respect

Roblox is a diverse community. We expect all users treat each other with respect and kindness, regardless of difference in identity, biliefs, or opinions.

Prohibited:

  • Bullying, harassment, or targeted attacks
  • Hate speech or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or disability
  • Sexual harassment or inappropriate advances
  • Impersonation or identity theft
  • Spam, scams, or phishing attempts
MODERATE TO SEVERE
SUSPENSION LIKELY

Integrity & Authenticity

We value honest and authentic interactions. Deceptive practices, cheating, and exploiting undermine the community experiences.

Prohibited:

  • Cheating, exploiting, or using unauthorized modifications
  • Account theft or unauthorized access
  • Selling or trading accounts
  • False reporting or abuse of moderation systems
  • Manipulating engagement or economy (botting, fake likes)
  • Intellectual property violations
MODERATE VIOLATIONS
ACCOUNT SUSPENSION

Content Standards

All content in Roblox-including games, avatars, audio, and text-must comply with our standards and be appreciate for our diverse community.

Prohibited:

  • Sexual or romantic content
  • Violent or gory content
  • Profanity and vulgar language
  • Political content intended to influence real-world elections
  • Gambling with real money or Robux
  • Advertising external services or off-platform transactions
  • Misleading or clickbait content
MINOR TO MODERATE
WARNING OR REMOVAL

ENFORCEMENT & CONSEQUENCES

Violations of our Community Standards result in moderation actions. The severity and frequency of violations determine by the consequences.

Warning

First-time minor violations typically receive a warning. The offending content is removed, and user is notified.

Temporary Suspensions

Moderate Violations or repeated minor violations result in temporary account suspension (1-4 days).

Account Deletion

Severe violations or repeated suspensions lead to permanent account termination with no possiblity of recovery.

IP/Device Ban

Extremely Severy violations may result in device or IP-Level bans, preventing creation of new accounts.

{% comment %} Appeal Process {% endcomment %}

APPEAL PROCESS

If you believe a moderation action was made in error, you have the right to appeal. All appeals are reviewed by our moderation team.

1

Review the Violation

Check your email for detail about moderation action, including which content or behavior violated our standards.

2

Submit an Appeal

Visit our Support page and submit an appeal within 30 days of the moderation action. Provide any relevant context or evidences.

3

Review Process

Our moderation team will review your proposal within 7-14 business days. You'll receive an email with the decision

4

Final Decision

Appeal decisions are final. If your appeal is denied, the original moderation action standards. If approved, any penalties are reversed.
