5. Attribution Requirements

If you use Roblox trademarks, logos, or branding in your projects, you must comply with our Brand Guidelines.

Permitted Uses

  • Factual references to Roblox in articles, videos, or educational materials
  • Displaying Roblox logo on promotional materials for games created on Roblox
  • Using "Made" with "Roblox" or "Built on Roblox" attributions

Prohibited Uses

  • Using Roblox branding in a way that implies official endorsement
  • Modifying or alterning Roblox logos or trademarks
  • Using Roblox branding for merchandise without permissions
  • Creating confusingly similar marks for logos

Requesting Permissions

For uses outside of these guidelines, please contact our legal team for permission at legal@roblox.com
