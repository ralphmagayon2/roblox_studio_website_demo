GUIDE FOR PARENT

Essential steps to ensure your child's safety on Roblox

1

Create a Parent Account

Set up your own Roblox account to better understand the platform and monitor your child's activity.

2

Set Age-Appropriate Settings

Configure account restrictions, privacy settings, and spending limits based on your child's age.

3

Enable Parental Controls

Use PIN protection, restrict chat features, and control who can communicate with your child.

4

Educate About Online Safety

Teach your child about not sharing personal information and how to report inappropriate behavior.

5

Review Account Activity

Regularly check your child's friends list, game history, and chat logs to stay informed.

6

Stay Involved

Play games together, discuss their experiences, and maintain open communication about their online interactions.

SAFETY TIPS

Best practices for staying safe online

Protect Your Password

Never share your password with anyone, including friends. Use a unique, strong password and enable two-factor authentication.

Keep Personal Info Private

Don't share your real name, address, phone number, school or other personal details with strangers online.

Be Cautious of Scams

Beware of offer that seem to good to be true. Never click suspicious links or download files from unknown sources.

Community Safety

Be respectful in all interactions. If someone makes your uncomfortable, block them immediately and report the behavior

Manage Spending

Set spending limits, use parental controls for purchases, and only buy Robux through official Roblox channels.

Choose Friends Carefully

Only accept friend request from people you know in real life. Be selective about who you trust online.
