Frequently Asked Questions

What is Robux? +
Robux is the virtual currency used in Roblox. It can be purchased with real money and used to buy in-game items, avatar accessories, and special abilities.
How do I purchase Robux? +
Select a package above and click "Buy Now". You can pay with credit card, PayPal, or other supported payment methods.
Can I earn Robux for free? +
Yes! Premium members receive monthly Robux. Creators can also earn Robux by selling game passes, items, and through the Developer Exchange program.
What is Roblox Premium? +
Roblox Premium is a monthly subscription that gives you a monthly Robux allowance, 10% more Robux on purchases, and exclusive benefits like trading and selling items.
