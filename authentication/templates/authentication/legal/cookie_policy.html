1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Purpose: Cookies help us understand how you use our services, remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and improve your overall experience on Roblox.

Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we use similar technologies including:

  • Web Beacons (Pixels): Small graphic images embedded in web pages or emails that track user activity
  • Local Storage: Technology that allows websites to store data locally in your browser
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage that expires when you close your browser
  • SDKs: Software development kits in mobile apps that collect usage data

For simplicity, we refer to all these technologies as "cookies" in this policy.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies to provide and improve our services. Below is a detailed breakdown:

Essential Cookies

REQUIRED

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

Examples: Login authentication, security tokens, session management

Performance Cookies

OPTIONAL

These cookies collect information about how you use our website, helping us understand which features are most popular and identify areas for improvement.

Examples: Page load times, error tracking, usage analytics

Functional Cookies

OPTIONAL

These cookies remember your choices and preferences to provide enhanced, personalized features and content.

Examples: Language preferences, theme settings, remembered username

Advertising Cookies

OPTIONAL

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

Examples: Ad targeting, conversion tracking, remarketing

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing and controlling cookies on your device:

5.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block only third-party cookies
  • Delete cookies after each browsing session
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

Browser Cookie Settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

5.2 Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of certain types of tracking:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Interest-Based Ads: Digital Advertising Alliance
  • Network Advertising: Network Advertising Initiative

5.3 Mobile Device Settings

For mobile devices, you can manage tracking through:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Important: Blocking or deleting essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features of our services, including logging in and accessing your account.
