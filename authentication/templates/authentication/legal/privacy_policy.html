1. Introduction

Welcome to Roblox's Privacy Policy. Roblox Corporation ("Roblox," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe online experience for all users.

Our Commitment: We are dedicated to being transparent about our data practices and giving you control over your personal information.

This Privacy Policy applies to all Roblox services, including our website, mobile applications, games, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Who We Are

Roblox Corporation is headquartered in San Mateo, California, USA. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, and Switzerland, Roblox serves as the data controller of your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

Information Type Examples Purpose
Account Information Username, password, email address, date of birth Account creation and authentication
Profile Information Avatar, bio, preferences Personalization and social features
Payment Information Credit card details, billing address Processing purchases
Communications Support tickets, chat messages, emails Customer support and platform moderation
User Content Games, models, scripts, images Providing platform services

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, IP address, browser type
  • Usage Information: Pages viewed, features used, games played, time spent, interaction patterns
  • Location Information: General location based on IP address (not precise geolocation)
  • Performance Data: Error reports, performance metrics, crash data

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third parties, including:

  • Social media platforms (if you connect your account)
  • Payment processors
  • Analytics providers
  • Parents or guardians (for users under 13)

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 Providing Services

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Enabling gameplay and social features
  • Processing transactions and payments
  • Delivering customer support
  • Personalizing your experience

3.2 Safety and Security

  • Monitoring and moderating content and behavior
  • Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and illegal activity
  • Protecting users, especially children
  • Enforcing our Terms of Use and Community Standards

3.3 Communication

  • Sending transactional messages (purchase confirmations, account updates)
  • Providing customer support
  • Sending marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Notifying you of platform updates and events

3.4 Improvement and Development

  • Analyzing usage patterns and trends
  • Developing new features and services
  • Conducting research and testing
  • Improving platform performance and reliability

4. Information Sharing

We Do Not Sell Your Data: Roblox does not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Other Users

Certain information is visible to other users, including your username, avatar, games you create, and in-game activities (subject to your privacy settings).

4.2 With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Payment processors
  • Customer support platforms
  • Analytics services
  • Marketing platforms

4.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Respond to government or law enforcement requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Enforce our Terms of Use
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activity

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

5.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us provide and improve our Services. We use both first-party cookies (set by Roblox) and third-party cookies (set by our partners).

5.2 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic platform functionality (login, account management)
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze usage and improve performance
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads (with your consent)

5.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, blocking essential cookies may impact your ability to use certain features.

6. Children's Privacy

COPPA Compliance: We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and take special measures to protect children's privacy.

6.1 Age Requirements

Users must be at least 13 years old to create a Roblox account. For users under 13, we require verifiable parental consent and implement additional privacy protections.

6.2 Parental Controls

Parents can:

  • Review and delete their child's account
  • Restrict who can communicate with their child
  • Limit spending and purchases
  • Receive monthly account activity summaries
  • Access and download their child's personal information

6.3 Limited Data Collection for Children

For users under 13, we limit data collection to what is necessary to provide the Services and comply with COPPA requirements.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)

Objection

Object to certain processing activities, such as marketing

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

Restriction

Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@roblox.com or use the privacy settings in your account. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Measures Include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication mechanisms (passwords, two-factor authentication)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures

Important: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Retention Criteria:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after deletion to prevent fraud and comply with legal obligations
  • Transaction Records: Retained as required by tax and accounting regulations (typically 7 years)
  • Support Communications: Retained for 3-5 years for quality assurance and legal compliance
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated data may be retained indefinitely for statistical purposes

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through your account settings or by contacting us.

10. International Data Transfers

Roblox is based in the United States, and your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

Data Transfer Mechanisms

For users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, we rely on the following mechanisms for international data transfers:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
  • Your explicit consent

These mechanisms ensure that your data receives adequate protection regardless of where it is processed.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors.

How We Notify You

When we make material changes, we will:

  • Post the updated Privacy Policy on our website
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Send an email notification to your registered email address
  • Display a prominent notice in the Services

Your continued use of the Services after the effective date of the updated Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.

Review Regularly: We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Contact Information

  • Phone: +1 (888) 858-2569

Supervisory Authority

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
